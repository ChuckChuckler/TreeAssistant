Tips

Planting trees can be great for the environment! But there are some important things you should keep in mind before doing so.

  • Getting Permission

    So you found some land to plant your tree. Excellent! But you should always contact the owner of the land and confirm your permissions with them.

  • Environment

    Remember that trees are living beings too, and they need their resources. Strive to find a place with nutritious soil and enough sun among other things.

  • Planting the Right Tree

    Not all trees are created equal. Keep in mind that planting non-native species, especially invasive ones, is not only illegal in some places but also horrible for the environment. Do thourough research on invasive species in your area-- it's great that you want to plant, but if you live in Texas and you're planting a Tree of Heaven, maybe hold off on that.
